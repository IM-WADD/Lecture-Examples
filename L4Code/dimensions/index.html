This Section Uses % for Margin and Padding

This section (black background) and its child aside (blue background) have padding set to 5% and margin set to 10%. This means the margin and padding of the aside will be smaller than those of the section because they are calculated as a percentage of the section width and height.

Resize the window and watch how the margins and padding change.

This Section Uses Viewport Units for Margin and Padding

This section (black background) and its child aside (blue background) have padding set to 5vh, 5 vw and margin set to 10vh and 10vw. This means the margin and padding of the aside will be the same as those of the section. The margin and padding in this section will be bigger than the margin and padding in the section above.

Resize the window and watch how the margins and padding change.
